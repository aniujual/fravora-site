What it does

Everything your idle screen deserves

🖼️

Smart Slideshow

Display photos from your device, network shares, Google Photos, OneDrive, or Dropbox — all in one seamless slideshow.

📻

Built-in Radio

Stream radio stations directly from the app while your photos play. Music and memories, together.

🌤️

Weather Overlay

Show live local weather right on your frame. Always know the forecast at a glance.

🎙️

Voice Control

Hands-free control with wake-phrase detection processed entirely on-device. No mic data ever leaves without your command.

📍

Metadata Overlay

Display the date and location each photo was taken, pulled from EXIF data — privately, locally, beautifully.

🔒

Privacy First

Your photos never leave your device. All credentials are encrypted locally. No personal data is collected or sold.

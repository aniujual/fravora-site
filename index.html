What it does

Everything your idle screen deserves

🖼️

Smart Slideshow

Display photos from local folders and SMB/NAS shares in one uninterrupted slideshow, with Pro unlocking larger libraries and recursive subfolder scans.

📻

Built-in Radio

Stream radio stations directly from the app while your photos play. Music and memories, together.

🌤️

Weather Overlay

Show live local weather right on your frame. Always know the forecast at a glance, without touching your phone.

🎙️

Voice Control

Hands-free control with wake-phrase detection processed entirely on-device. No mic data ever leaves without your command.

📍

Metadata Overlay

Display the date and location each photo was taken, pulled from EXIF data — privately, locally, beautifully.

⏰

Smart Scheduling

Set the frame to turn on and off automatically. It works when you need it, sleeps when you don't.

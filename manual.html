User Manual

Everything you need to set up, configure, and get the most out of Fravora — from your first photo source to advanced voice control.

Last updated: May 18, 2026 · Based on current app implementation
🚀
01

Quick Start

From install to running slideshow in five steps:

1
Open Fravora and complete onboarding. You can replay it anytime from Settings → Support → Replay Onboarding.
2
Go to Settings → Sources and add at least one photo source — local folder, SMB/NAS share, or Google Photos.
3
Go to Settings → Albums, create an album, and attach the source folders or collections you added.
4
In Settings → Slideshow, set your preferred photo interval, display mode, and optional running hours.
5
Return to the frame view. The slideshow starts automatically once images are found.
💡
If nothing appears, check the in-app guidance cards — they explain missing albums, empty sources, or sources that haven't been scanned yet.
🗂️
02

Sources

Sources define where your photos come from. Fravora supports local folders, network shares, and cloud services. Add sources first — albums reference them.

Network Shares (SMB / NAS)

Connect to any SMB-compatible NAS or shared folder on your local network.

Host / ShareEnter in hostname/sharename format — no smb:// prefix needed.
CredentialsUsername and password are optional — leave blank for guest access.
FoldersAfter connecting, select one or more folders from the share. A share with no selected folders is inactive.
Network Retry CountHow many times Fravora retries a failed image load before marking it unavailable. Increase for unstable networks.

Local Folders

Add folders stored directly on the device using the system file picker. You'll be prompted to grant folder access permission.

✦
PRO: Albums can reference multiple local folders. Free tier is limited to one local folder per album.

Cloud Sources

☁️
Google Photos
Connect your Google account and select albums or photo picks. Free tier supports 1 album; PRO expands the quota and syncs more frequently.
📁
OneDrive
Connect your Microsoft account and select cloud folders to include in your slideshow.

Cache & Storage

Image Cache SizeControls how much local storage is used for resized image cache. Larger values reduce re-loads; smaller values save space.
Google Photos headroomReserves free-space on your device before downloading Google Photos images, protecting against storage exhaustion.
Clear CacheAvailable for both standard image cache and Google Photos cache. Useful if old picks are no longer needed or the cache grows too large.
🖼️
03

Albums

Albums are the playback containers Fravora scans for slideshow images. You can have multiple albums and enable or disable each independently.

Create albumGive the album a name, then attach SMB folders, local folders, and/or cloud collections to it.
Enable for playbackOnly enabled albums are included in the slideshow. Toggle albums on/off without deleting them.
Include subfoldersRecursively scan nested folders within the attached source. PRO required for SMB recursive scanning.
Image countersEach album shows visible / hidden / total image counts so you can verify the configuration.
Hidden photosHide individual photos per-album or globally without deleting them. Unhide anytime from Settings → Albums → Hidden photos.
⚠️
If all photos in your enabled albums are hidden, Fravora will show nothing. Go to Hidden photos to unhide some images.
💡
When importing from Google Photos, Fravora warns you if any selected picks are already hidden and excludes them automatically.

Overlays

Overlays are elements displayed on top of your photos during the slideshow. Each can be positioned, sized, and toggled independently.

🕐
Clock
Shows the current time. Configurable size and time/date format. Always available on Free.
🌤️
Weather
Live local weather conditions overlaid on the frame. Requires location permission.
📍
Photo Info
Shows the location and date each photo was taken, read from EXIF data. Processed locally.
🎵
Artist & Song Info
Displays the artist and track name while radio is playing.
📻
Radio Indicator
A small indicator showing the currently playing station.
🔢
Photo Counter
Shows your position in the album (e.g. 12 / 340). Placeable at top or bottom.
🌀
Ambient Logo
A subtle Fravora logo watermark over the frame.
🛡️
Anti Burn-In
Periodically micro-shifts overlays and varies opacity to protect OLED displays.
✦
Overlay Layout (PRO): Drag overlays into any of 6 screen positions (top-left, top-center, top-right, bottom-left, bottom-center, bottom-right) or hide them entirely.
💡
On Free, the clock is fixed to the bottom-right. The position controls are visible but locked until PRO is active.

Basic Playback (Free)

SearchSearch for stations by name, genre, language, or country. Free tier has a daily search limit.FREE

08

Voice Control

✦
PRO feature. Voice control requires microphone permission and an Android speech recognition service (typically Speech Services by Google).

Setup

1
Make sure Speech Services by Google (or another speech recognition service) is installed and set as the device voice input in Android Settings → General Management → Language and Input.
2
In Fravora, go to Settings → Voice and grant microphone permission when prompted.
3
Enable the Voice Control master toggle, then enable individual feature toggles (radio, slideshow, assistant) as needed.
4
Choose your wake phrase and optional wake feedback sound. Say the wake phrase — you'll see a blue listening frame.

Wake Phrases

Hello FrameDefault wake phrase. Processed entirely on-device using OpenWakeWord.
Hello FravoraAlternative wake phrase. Same on-device processing.

Commands

Play / start / resume radioStarts or resumes the radio stream.
Stop / pause radioPauses or stops the current radio stream.
Play / pause / stop slideshowControls slideshow playback without touching the screen.
Disable voice controlTurns off voice listening until re-enabled in Settings.

Visual Feedback

Blue frameFravora is listening for a command.
Green frame + soundCommand was recognized and acted on.
Red frame + soundSpeech recognition is unavailable or failed.
💡
If no station is configured and you say "play radio", Fravora opens Settings → Radio automatically so you can pick one.

Troubleshooting

No photos are showing
  • Confirm at least one album exists and is enabled for playback.
  • Check that the album has at least one source folder attached.
  • If photos are in nested folders, enable Include subfolders (PRO for SMB).
  • Wait for the initial scan to complete — large folders take a moment.
All photos are hidden / slideshow is blank
Go to Settings → Albums → Hidden photos and unhide the images you want to show. Album counters display visible/hidden/total counts to help identify the issue.
SMB / NAS share won't connect
  • Verify the host/share path — use hostname/sharename format with no smb:// prefix.
  • Check your credentials. Try guest access (blank username/password) if the share allows it.
  • Increase Network Retry Count in Sources for unstable network links.
  • Ensure the device is on the same local network as the NAS.
Google Photos won't sync or import is blocked
  • Free up storage space on the device.
  • Lower the minimum free-space headroom setting in Sources → Google Photos.
  • Clear the Google Photos cache from Sources settings if old picks are stale.
Radio station won't play
Fravora shows the specific failure reason: timeout, network issue, access denied, unavailable stream, or unsupported format. Try a different station if the stream is down, or check your internet connection for network issues.
Voice control won't enable
  • Install Speech Services by Google from the Play Store if it's not already present.
  • Set it as the default voice input: Android Settings → General Management → Language and Input → Voice Input.
  • Grant microphone permission to Fravora when prompted.
Screen keeps turning off during slideshow
Enable Keep Screen Awake in Settings → Appearance. Also check your device's display timeout setting — some Android versions override app-level wake locks at aggressive timeout values.
PRO features disappeared after an ad session expired
Temporary PRO access from rewarded ads expires after the granted time (+15 min per ad, up to 4/day). Your PRO settings are saved and will be automatically restored the next time you activate PRO — either via another ad or a permanent purchase.
